Important information about the nature and limitations of our assessments.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Not Clinical Assessment

The assessments provided by SIGNATUREWITHIN — including all personality, EQ, temperament, money mindset, relationship, leadership, career, and marriage tools — are self-discovery instruments for personal development purposes only.

They are not clinical psychological assessments, psychiatric evaluations, or diagnostic tools. They should not be used as a substitute for professional psychological evaluation, therapy, counseling, or medical advice.

Accuracy of Results

Assessment results reflect your self-reported responses at the time of taking the assessment. Results can be influenced by your current emotional state, life circumstances, and the honesty with which you answer. They represent one data point — a reflection tool — not a fixed or permanent characterization of who you are.

Human personality, emotional intelligence, and behavioral patterns are complex and not fully captured by any assessment tool. Our results are a starting point for reflection, not a complete picture.

Not Professional Advice

Nothing on SIGNATUREWITHIN constitutes:

  • Professional psychological or psychiatric advice
  • Medical diagnosis or treatment recommendations
  • Legal advice
  • Financial or investment advice
  • Marriage or couples counseling
  • Career counseling or professional guidance

If you are dealing with significant mental health challenges, relationship crisis, financial difficulty, or career distress, please seek qualified professional support.

Marriage Metrics Specific Notice

The Marriage Metrics assessment is designed for couples who are generally functioning and seeking growth — not for situations involving abuse, active crisis, or severe dysfunction. If you are in an unsafe relationship, please contact appropriate support services.

Results Are Not Permanent

People grow and change. An assessment result from today is not a permanent label. We encourage you to retake assessments periodically and use your results as a tool for intentional development — not a fixed identity.
