Privacy Policy

How SIGNATUREWITHIN collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to operate the site and deliver your report. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. Your assessment answers are used only to generate your results.

Who We Are

SIGNATUREWITHIN is a self-discovery platform offering personality, EQ, relationship, career, and marriage assessments. Our website is signaturewithin.com.

Information We Collect

Assessment Data

When you take an assessment, your answers are processed to generate your results. This data is stored temporarily in your browser session. We do not permanently store individual question responses on our servers.

Payment Information

When you purchase a full report ($14.99), payment is processed by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of payment data.

Email Address

If you subscribe to our weekly insights email, we collect your email address. We use Brevo to manage our email list. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Analytics Data

We use Vercel Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This collects anonymized data including page views, referral sources, and device type. No personally identifiable information is collected by our analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your assessment results and PDF report
  • To send you the weekly insights email you subscribed to
  • To understand how the site is being used so we can improve it
  • To process your payment securely through Stripe

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share your information with advertisers
  • We do not use your assessment answers for any purpose other than generating your results
  • We do not track you across other websites

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function — primarily for payment session management via Stripe. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Data Retention

Assessment session data is retained only for the duration of your session plus a short window to allow PDF generation after payment. Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Payment records are retained as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To make such a request, contact us at the address below.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Brevo — Email list management (brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy)
  • Vercel — Hosting and analytics (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Groq — AI report generation (groq.com/privacy-policy)

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@signaturewithin.com
