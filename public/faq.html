Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about taking assessments and getting your report.

The Assessments

Are the assessments really free?

Yes. Every assessment on SIGNATUREWITHIN is free to take, start to finish. You see your complete results — your scores, your type, your profile — before any payment is requested. You only pay $14.99 if you want the full personalized PDF report.

How long does each assessment take?

Most assessments take 8–15 minutes depending on the number of questions. The Temperament Profile, EQ Report, Know Thy Self, Relationship Style, Stress Pattern Analysis, and Marriage Metrics each have 60 questions and take approximately 12 minutes. The Money Mindset and Entrepreneur Metrics have 40 questions and take about 8 minutes. The Ideas Validator has 50 questions and takes about 10 minutes.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to take any assessment or see your results. You can complete the entire experience — including purchasing your report — without creating an account.

Can I take the same assessment more than once?

Yes. People change, and assessment results reflect who you are at the time of taking it. We recommend retaking assessments every 6-12 months to track your development over time.

Should I answer how I am now or how I want to be?

Always answer how you actually are right now — not your ideal self, not your best self, not how you used to be. The assessment is most useful when it reflects your current honest reality. A result that flatters you is less valuable than a result that is accurate.

The Reports

What is in the full PDF report?

Every report is approximately 10 pages and includes: your complete score breakdown across all dimensions, an AI-generated personalized interpretation of your specific profile, your 2-3 primary strengths explained in depth, your 2-3 priority growth areas with specific practices, how your profile shows up in your relationships and work, and a 30 or 90-day growth plan with daily practices designed for your specific results.

How do I download my report?

After payment, your report opens immediately in a new browser tab. It is formatted for printing — use your browser's print function (File → Print) and select "Save as PDF" to save it to your device. No email delivery is required.

What if my report did not generate after payment?

This occasionally happens due to browser settings or connection issues. First, check that the new tab did not open in the background. If the report truly did not generate, email us at hello@signaturewithin.com with your order details and we will resolve it promptly.

Are reports refundable?

Because reports are generated and delivered immediately upon payment, we do not offer refunds once a report has been generated. If you experience a technical issue with delivery, contact us and we will make it right.

Is my report stored anywhere?

Your report is generated in the moment and delivered directly to your browser. We recommend saving it immediately after generation. We do not store your completed report on our servers beyond the generation session.

Marriage Metrics

Can my spouse and I take it together?

The Marriage Metrics assessment has separate Husband and Wife versions — each with gender-specific questions. Each spouse takes their own version independently and receives their own personalized report. You can then compare results if you choose, though each report is designed to stand alone.

Do we need to share our results with each other?

That is entirely your choice. Many couples find value in discussing their results together. Others prefer to work through their own report privately first. Neither approach is wrong — use the tool in whatever way creates the most honest reflection for you.

Privacy and Payment

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe — one of the world's most trusted payment processors. We never see, store, or have access to your credit card information.

What do you do with my email address?

If you subscribe to our weekly insights email, we use your email address only to send that newsletter. We do not share it with third parties or use it for any other purpose. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Still Have Questions?

Email us at hello@signaturewithin.com and we will respond within 24 hours.
