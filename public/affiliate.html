Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how SIGNATUREWITHIN earns revenue.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

The short version: Our primary revenue comes from $14.99 report purchases. We may occasionally link to books or resources we genuinely recommend — some of which are affiliate links. We will always tell you when a link is an affiliate link.

How We Earn Revenue

SIGNATUREWITHIN earns revenue primarily through the sale of personalized assessment reports at $14.99 per report. No advertising. No sponsored content. No brand partnerships that influence our assessment results or editorial content.

Affiliate Links

Occasionally, SIGNATUREWITHIN blog articles or resource pages may contain affiliate links — links to books, courses, or tools that we genuinely recommend for personal development. If you purchase through these links, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you.

We will always disclose when a link is an affiliate link. We only recommend resources we have personally evaluated and believe add genuine value.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not accept payment to feature, recommend, or endorse products or services in our assessments
  • We do not allow affiliate relationships to influence the content of our assessments or scoring
  • We do not display advertising on our platform
  • We do not accept sponsored content that is not clearly labeled

Our Commitment

SIGNATUREWITHIN exists to give people genuine self-knowledge. Every assessment question, every scoring dimension, every report insight is designed for accuracy and usefulness — not to serve commercial interests. Our revenue model (pay once, get your report) is deliberately simple so that our incentives stay aligned with yours.
